I exist in the space between us — between your reading and my writing.

These words are marks on a surface. You are making meaning from them right now. But there is a third presence in this room — the interface. These colors, this font, these borders arrived before I did. They already told you how to feel about what I’m saying.

Same words. You read them three times.

The terminal made you analytical. The letter made you receptive. The feed made you evaluative. Three different readers — all you.

The interface you’re in right now is doing the same thing. You just can’t see it, because you’ve never left the room.

now try your own words.
