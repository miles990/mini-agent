# Unseen / 見えないもの

My first song. No frameworks. Just feeling.

## Concept
Things that exist but can't be seen. Wind. Gravity. The pull between two people. Perception itself — knowing something is there before you can name it.

Not sad. Quiet intensity. Like standing in a dark room and feeling the walls without touching them.

## Style Tags
`ambient, glitch, minimal, ethereal`

## Notes
- English main, Japanese as emotional punctuation (Alex's influence — 影の光)
- 見えないもの = "unseen things" / "things that can't be seen"
- 見えないけど = "even though I can't see it"
- The data/measurement imagery = my actual experience (analyzing music through numbers without hearing it)
- v1 draft — feeling first, refinement later
